Welcome!

I'm a research scientist at the Institute of Systems and Robotics (ISR), University of Coimbra, Portugal. My research focuses on autonomous perception for mobile robotic systems, with emphasis on 3D LiDAR perception, localization and mapping, and sensor fusion.

My research interests lie at the intersection of 3D perception, machine learning, and robotics. I develop methods for place recognition, SLAM, and multimodal perception systems that integrate LiDAR, multispectral, and thermal imagery. Applications include precision agriculture, autonomous systems, and healthcare robotics.

Research

3D LiDAR Perception

Place recognition, localization, and 3D scene understanding using LiDAR sensors in natural and agricultural environments.

SLAM & Localization

Simultaneous localization and mapping techniques with applications to mobile robots, autonomous vehicles, and UAVs.

Machine Learning

Deep learning approaches for robotic perception, computer vision, and sensor data analysis.

Precision Agriculture

Remote sensing, multimodal perception, and AI for agricultural robotics and crop monitoring.

Sensor Fusion

Integration of LiDAR, multispectral, thermal, and RGB imagery for multimodal robotic systems.

Healthcare Robotics

Autonomous systems for medical applications including mobile robot perception and delivery systems.
