About Me

I am a research scientist at the Institute of Systems and Robotics (ISR), University of Coimbra, Portugal. My research focuses on 3D perception with LiDAR, machine learning, localization, and mapping, with applications in both agricultural and urban environments.

I have developed methods for place recognition, SLAM, and sensor fusion, as well as multimodal perception systems that integrate LiDAR, multispectral, and thermal imagery. My work has been published in leading conferences and journals, including ITSC, IROS, RA-L, and Computers and Electronics in Agriculture.

Research interests: Robotics, 3D perception, machine learning, precision agriculture, autonomous systems, and sensor fusion.

Research Focus Areas

🔍 3D LiDAR Perception

Place recognition, localization, and 3D scene understanding using LiDAR sensors in natural and agricultural environments.

🗺️ SLAM & Mapping

Simultaneous localization and mapping techniques with applications to mobile robots, autonomous vehicles, and UAVs.

🤖 Machine Learning

Deep learning approaches for robotic perception, computer vision, and sensor data analysis with focus on interpretability.

🌾 Precision Agriculture

Remote sensing, multimodal perception, and AI for agricultural robotics, crop monitoring, and soil management.

📡 Sensor Fusion

Integration of LiDAR, multispectral, thermal, and RGB imagery for multimodal robotic perception systems.

🏥 Healthcare Robotics

Autonomous systems for medical applications including mobile robot perception and autonomous delivery systems.
